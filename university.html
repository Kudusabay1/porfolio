Harvard University



Harvard

The school that has caught my eye is Harvard. There is a reason everyone has heard. It is a good school. I am drawn to Harvard for its unmatched commitment to academic excellence. My educational goals are perfectly aligned with the university's emphasis on encouraging critical thinking, innovation, and global viewpoints. Harvard promises an extensiveand enriching education that goes beyond the classroom thanks to its rich history, active campus life, and wealth of resources. Joining a group of motivated and successful people, having access to cutting edge research, and having a strong alumni network make Harvard the perfect place for me to learn and develop the skills I'll need for success in the future and to give back to society.

Requirements

Harvard's application requirements typically include a completed Common Application or Universal College Application, high school transcripts, standardized test scores like SAT and ACT, letters of recommendation, a personal essay, and an application fee or fee waiver. Additionally, demonstrating excellence in academics, extracurricular activities, and personal achievements can enhance your application. You also have to pay an annual tuition for Harvard University which is around $51,925. Along with additional expenses such as room and board, books, and personal expenses.
